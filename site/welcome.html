You're in!

Check your email for your API key. Here's how to make your first proof.

1 Check your email

We sent your API key to your email. It looks like tzk_....

Don't see it? Check spam, or contact support.

2 Make your first proof

Prove that a secret number is in a range — without revealing the number:

curl -X POST https://api.tinyzkp.com/prove/template/range_proof \ -H "Authorization: Bearer tzk_YOUR_KEY" \ -H "Content-Type: application/json" \ -d '{"params":{"min":0,"max":100,"witness_steps":[20,22]}}'

3 Verify it (free)

Verify the proof — free, no charge. Auth is required to prevent abuse:

curl -X POST https://api.tinyzkp.com/verify \ -H "Authorization: Bearer tzk_YOUR_KEY" \ -H "Content-Type: application/json" \ -d '{"proof": YOUR_PROOF_OBJECT}'

Response: {"valid": true, "verified_in_ms": 2.8}
