Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What We Collect

  • Account information: Email address provided during signup.
  • Usage data: API requests (tenant ID, trace length, duration, timestamp). We do not store program contents or proof data after delivery.
  • Billing data: Processed by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers.
  • Client-side verification: The @tinyzkp/verify WASM package runs entirely in your browser or runtime. No proof data, verification results, or telemetry are sent to TinyZKP servers during client-side verification.

2. How We Use It

  • To provide and bill the Service.
  • To monitor service health and prevent abuse.
  • To communicate about your account.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Stripe for payment processing.
  • Law enforcement when legally required.

4. Data Retention

Usage logs are retained for billing and audit purposes. Account data is retained while your account is active and for 90 days after deletion.

5. Security

We use TLS encryption for all API traffic, authenticated access via API keys, and follow security best practices for data storage.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data via our contact form.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. We will notify you of material changes via email.

8. Contact

Questions? Contact us.
